360 Degrees of Reliance

Toshiba's 360° Reliance program puts customers first, by providing 360 degrees of personal attention.

With 24/7 proactive monitoring, access and support, Toshiba provides immediate access to service and support teams with a commitment to prompt resolutions.

360° Reliance makes it easy to do business with Toshiba. Tools like the 360° Connect and Vitality Solutions help you with total asset and practice management. And Envision 360 helps you plan your equipment and room needs to enhance your clinical workflows for better patient experiences.
